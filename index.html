Getting Started

Here are some workouts that
can improve your overall fitness!

3
Basic
Tips
To start
your
Routine

Start Slow

Don't push yourself too hard at the beginning of your workout routine.

Warm-Up and Cool Down!

Before starting your workout, spend a few minutes warming up to get your muscles ready for exercise.

Stay Consistent

The most important thing when starting a workout routine is to stay consistent.

6
Basic
Beginner
Workouts

Bodyweight Squats

This exercise helps to build strength in your lower body.

Push-ups

Push-ups are a great exercise to help you build upper body strength, specifically your chest, triceps, and shoulders.

Lunges

Lunges help to strengthen your lower body, specifically your glutes, hamstrings, and quads.

Plank

Planks help to strengthen your core muscles, specifically your abs and lower back.

Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks are a great way to get your heart rate up and improve your cardiovascular health.

Burpees

Burpees are a full-body exercise that can help to improve your strength, endurance, and cardiovascular health.

Leafy Green Vegetables

Leafy greens like spinach, kale, and Swiss chard are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Lean Protein Sources

Incorporate lean protein sources into your diet, such as chicken breast, turkey, fish, tofu, beans, and lentils.

Whole Grain

Choose whole grains like quinoa, brown rice, oats, and whole wheat bread.

Healthy Fats

Include healthy fats in your diet, such as avocados, nuts (like almonds and walnuts), seeds (like chia and flaxseeds), and olive oil.

Colorful Fruits and Vegetables

Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your meals.
